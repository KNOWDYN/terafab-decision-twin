Terafab consequences need a model, not just a narrative

Terafab Decision Twin turns declared assumptions into auditable consequences across power, cooling, water, yield, cost, governance, legitimacy, and readiness.

Open repository
What stakeholders get
  • Capital risk visible before commitment.
  • Physical and policy constraints in one frame.
  • Evidence labels separating facts, assumptions, and unknowns.

The problem

Power, water, heat, yield, cost, and legitimacy do not fail in isolation.

A terafab-scale story can sound plausible while hiding the first binding constraint. The brief keeps coupled risks visible before sunk-cost momentum hardens the plan.

Powerfirm capacity, load factor, cost, emissions
Heatrejection load, cooling reserve, exergy loss
Waterwithdrawal, consumption, UPW, wastewater
Valueyield, readiness, capex, opex, gate action

The trust engine

Credibility of first principles and public information

Inputs are evidence-coded before analysis begins. Unknowns remain visible. Assumptions can drive scenarios without becoming verified operating facts.

Evidence labels

Verified, filed or reported, user-provided, scenario assumption, stress test, unknown, confidential input, and derived output stay distinct.

Non-promotion rule

Scenario assumptions, reports, uncertainty ranges, and confidential inputs are not promoted into verified project facts.

Unknown discipline

Unknown values are not silently filled into conclusions unless the scenario explicitly allows substitution.

Restricted-source boundary

Public outputs do not redistribute private, confidential, or restricted source documents.

Decision surface

One model, four stakeholder views.

The decision surface compresses uncertainty, strategy, reduced-order dynamics, and validation readiness into views that different stakeholders can act on.

01Board view

Gate-failure probability, dominant gate risks, recommended phase posture.

02Investor view

Cost distribution medians and strategic conflict signals.

03Policy view

Water, wastewater, legitimacy, and public-benefit risk indicators.

04Research view

Run counts, game profiles, trajectory steps, and validation level.

How it works

Declared assumptions in. Gate-aware decisions out.

The model validates scenario JSON, audits evidence status, expands time steps, runs formal equation modules, and emits outputs that keep blockers visible.

  1. Validatescenario structure, evidence metadata, unknowns
  2. Computepower, cooling, water, yield, economics, policy
  3. Gatefirm power, water, cooling, readiness, legitimacy
  4. ReportJSON, Markdown, CSV, warnings, assumptions used

Who it helps

Different stakeholders can argue from the same decision surface.

Boards

See which gate blocks commitment and whether the next posture is advance, hold, redesign, stage, contract, curtail, or abandon.

Investors

Stress cost per output through energy, water, delay, yield, capex, and strategic conflict exposure.

Policymakers

Screen public infrastructure pressure before incentives or community commitments are framed as settled.

Engineers

Trace whether power, cooling, water, throughput, or readiness becomes the first hard limit.

Researchers

Use bounded, reproducible scenarios without confusing public analysis for private project truth.

Validation readiness

Screening is not the same as official validation.

The included validation lab supports code/schema execution, structural validation, and public-reference screening. Benchmark, expert, official, or calibrated validation requires additional evidence.

Required boundaries

The site is designed to build trust without implying private authority.

This is an independent KNOWDYN work. It is source-available, not open source, and does not claim Terafab endorsement, authorization, confidential access, official project status, permitting conclusions, or investment advice.

Interpretation rule

Outputs are scenario-dependent analytical estimates derived from declared assumptions, model structure, and available public or user-supplied inputs. They must not be presented as verified operating facts without independent validation.
