LaTeX-rendered architecture

The model converts fab-scale physics into decision gates.

Equations are public, readable, and explicitly separated from undisclosed or unverified operating facts.

First-law power balance

\[\dot{Q}_{remove}=P_{IT}+P_{process}+P_{aux}-\dot{W}_{useful}-\Delta \dot{U}\]

Identifies thermal burden and cooling demand from scenario-level loads.

Exergy destruction

\[\dot{X}_{destroyed}=T_0\dot{S}_{gen}\]

Converts entropy generation into lost work potential and thermodynamic inefficiency.

Water-cooling coupling

\[\dot{m}_{water}=\frac{\dot{Q}_{remove}}{c_p\Delta T\eta_{cooling}}\]

Stress-tests withdrawal, consumption, and cooling-system sensitivity.

Yield-adjusted economic output

\[V_{net}=Y \cdot V_{gross}-C_{energy}-C_{water}-C_{delay}-C_{risk}\]

Links production value to utility burden, readiness risk, and delays.

Evidence gate

\[Claim_{public}\leftarrow Claim \; | \; status \in \{verified,filed,reported,input,assumption,unknown\}\]

Prevents unverified claims from entering public outputs as facts.

Real-option value

\[ROV = \max(E[V_{stage+1}]-I_{stage},0)\cdot \pi_{readiness}\]

Frames staged decisions under uncertainty rather than irreversible overcommitment.

Interactive model map

Touch a module to understand its executive function.

The model is modular so engineers can audit equations while executives read decision consequences.
