Evidence and independence

The model earns credibility by refusing to overclaim.

Inputs are labeled before analysis begins. Outputs are derived consequences, not automatic facts about any real Terafab facility.

Verified

Publicly supportable or admitted information.

Filed or reported

Traceable claims preserved as claims, not independently verified facts.

Assumption

Scenario-provided modeling values used to test consequences.

Unknown

Not inferred, not filled from restricted sources, and not hidden.

Required boundary: Terafab, the Terafab name, and related marks or business identity belong to their rightful owners. This KNOWDYN site and package are independent and do not claim endorsement, authorization, employment, agency, partnership, acquisition interest, confidential access, approval, or involvement by Terafab or its representatives.

Source governance

Restricted source documents are not redistributed or exposed.

The public asset is the framing, schema, equations, scenario discipline, validation tooling, and audit trail—not private-data insinuation.
