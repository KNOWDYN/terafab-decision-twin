Policy intelligence

A decision surface for grid, water, industrial readiness, and semiconductor strategy.

The site turns model outputs into questions that policymakers and infrastructure planners can use before committing capital, incentives, water allocation, or grid capacity.

Grid planners

How much firm capacity, transmission, storage, cooling power, and demand flexibility would a Terafab-scale scenario require?

Outputs: load, energy, peak stress, cooling burden.

Water authorities

How do reuse fraction, cooling technology, ambient conditions, and production intensity change regional water stress?

Outputs: withdrawal, consumption, reuse-adjusted demand.

Industrial-policy teams

Which assumptions dominate readiness risk, qualification delay, subsidy exposure, and national-capacity benefit?

Outputs: readiness, gates, option value, sensitivity.

Terafab strategists

Which public assumptions should be corrected, confidentially bounded, or translated into official operating scenarios?

Outputs: evidence gaps, priority data requests, policy narrative.

Positioning boundary

This site is built to be strategically useful to Terafab-scale stakeholders, but it does not claim endorsement, validation, acquisition interest, proprietary access, or verified operating data.

Terafab Decision Twin

Public, evidence-gated decision support for Terafab-scale semiconductor energy, water, exergy, yield, economic, governance, and policy analysis.

Boundaries

No Terafab endorsement is claimed. Scenario assumptions are not verified operating facts. Restricted sources are not redistributed.
