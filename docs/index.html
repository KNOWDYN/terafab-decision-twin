Rendered equations

The model is mathematical, but the site stays readable.

Thermal removal

\[\dot Q_{remove}=P_{IT}+P_{process}+P_{aux}-\dot W_{useful}-\Delta\dot U\]

Turns facility power into cooling burden.

Exergy loss

\[\dot X_{destroyed}=T_0\dot S_{gen}\]

Quantifies lost work potential caused by irreversibility.

Cooling water

\[\dot m_{water}=\frac{\dot Q_{remove}}{c_p\Delta T_{cooling}}\]

Connects heat rejection to withdrawal and consumption stress.

Net value

\[V_{net}=YV_{gross}-C_{energy}-C_{water}-C_{delay}-C_{risk}\]

Links physical limits to economic outcome.

Why it matters

Terafab-scale planning needs one public place where physics, finance, and policy meet.

Large semiconductor manufacturing projects can stress grids, water systems, permitting schedules, and capital allocation at the same time. A single narrative cannot manage those dependencies.

This site explains the public model. The repository contains the runnable package, schema, scenarios, tests, and evidence gates.
