Terafab-scale decisions need a model before they need a narrative.

The Terafab Decision Twin converts declared assumptions into auditable consequences for power, cooling, water, yield, cost, governance, legitimacy, and readiness.

It is not a claim of Terafab endorsement, private data access, or verified operating performance. It is a public decision instrument for disciplined review.

Decision surface

One public lens for the constraints that can stop a megafab.

The site is intentionally direct: it shows which coupled variables matter, why they matter, and how the repository computes them.

Power

Firm capacity margin, load factor, emissions, and energy cost.

Heat

First-law heat rejection, cooling reserve, entropy, and exergy loss.

Water

Withdrawal, consumption, wastewater, UPW demand, and permit margin.

Value

Yield, readiness, capex, opex, legitimacy, and phase-gate action.

Operating principle

Assumptions go in labeled. Consequences come out auditable.

Validate scenario JSON

Required sections and evidence-coded material inputs are checked before simulation.

Run equation modules

Power, cooling, water, manufacturing, economics, policy, and gate modules compute outputs.

Report decision readiness

Outputs are summarized with warning flags, assumptions used, and reproducibility metadata.

Executive boundary: the model is useful because it is explicit. Scenario results are not verified Terafab operating facts unless independently validated.
