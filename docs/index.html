Stakeholder fit

Different readers, one shared decision surface.

The package is built for people who need to discuss Terafab-scale infrastructure without flattening engineering, finance, and policy into separate stories.

Leadership

Use it to compare phase-gate readiness, capital timing, utility bottlenecks, and public-exposure tradeoffs.

Engineering

Inspect load, heat rejection, cooling reserve, water withdrawal, wastewater, yield, contamination, and qualification assumptions.

Investors

Stress cost per good die, cost per compute watt, capex, opex, delay exposure, and downside gates.

Policy teams

Translate grid, water, emissions, public benefit, and legitimacy into a concrete planning conversation.

How it works

Scenario in. Auditable consequences out.

Declare the case

Choose a horizon, scenario type, and material assumptions across energy, cooling, water, manufacturing, economics, governance, and policy.

Label the evidence

Every material input carries value, unit, status, source reference, confidence, and notes.

Run the model

The engine computes physical, economic, policy, and readiness outputs with gates and reports.

Power

Firm capacity margin, load factor, emissions, electricity cost.

Water

Withdrawal, consumption, wastewater, permit reserve, UPW demand.

Readiness

Yield, qualification, packaging, governance risk, phase-gate action.
