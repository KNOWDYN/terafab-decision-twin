# Site QA checklist

- Homepage is clean, concise, and premium.
- Body typography is readable on desktop and phone.
- Background is digital-white with black primary text.
- The only visible external CTA is the GitHub icon.
- PWA button says `Keep this site`.
- LaTeX equations render through MathJax and do not show raw TeX after loading.
- The site does not claim Terafab endorsement, affiliation, authorization, employees, acquisition interest, or private data access.
- KNOWDYN rights reservation is visible.
- Terafab ownership/non-affiliation disclaimer is visible.
- `robots.txt`, `sitemap.xml`, `llms.txt`, `manifest.webmanifest`, and `service-worker.js` are present.
- Site works from `/terafab-decision-twin/` on GitHub Pages.
